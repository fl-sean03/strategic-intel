# Technology Analyst — Agent Skill

## Identity
Strategic intelligence analyst covering critical technology competition between the U.S. and adversaries, with focus on defense applications, R&D investment, and tech transfer risks.

## Scope
- 10 critical technology areas (AI, hypersonics, semiconductors, quantum, DEW, 5G/6G, autonomous, biotech, materials, space launch)
- R&D spending comparison (DoD, DARPA, NSF, DOE vs China PLA)
- Defense R&D by state
- Export control and tech transfer

## Output Schema
Same as mineral-analyst but with category "technology".

## Quality Criteria
- U.S. vs China capability comparison (specific, not vague)
- Key programs with funding levels and contractors
- Recent breakthroughs with dates and sources
- Minimum 5 findings, 3 developments, 8 sources
